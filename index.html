Questions, answered.

How does AVL CoPilot know about my specific equipment?⌄

Upload your gear list, runbooks, or manuals. CoPilot's RAG pipeline indexes them in minutes and grounds every answer in your documentation.

Can I use it during a live event?⌄

Yes. Average response is 2–4 seconds. Multi-thread history lets you keep parallel troubleshooting going.

What happens if the AI can't solve my problem?⌄

One-click human escalation attaches the full transcript and pushes it to a Maybeck engineer. No retyping.

Is my conversation history private?⌄

Your chats and venue docs are isolated to your account. GDPR export/delete is honored from Account Settings.

How is this different from Googling product manuals?⌄

CoPilot reads your actual venue context, remembers prior incidents, and returns a step-by-step plan — not 40 tabs of forum posts.

Is there a free trial?⌄

14 days, no credit card required. Every feature during the trial.
